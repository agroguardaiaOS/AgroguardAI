Capabilities

What the model can do

Agroguard Intelligence is purpose-built for agriculture, combining deep domain knowledge with multimodal understanding.

Agricultural Knowledge

Deep understanding of 500+ crop varieties, growth cycles, nutrient requirements, and optimal cultivation across diverse climatic regions.

Multimodal Vision

Process images of crops, soil, pests, and diseases. Identify problems from a single photo with 90%+ accuracy on Nigerian crops.

Multilingual NLP

Integrations

Build with Agroguard Intelligence

RESTful API with SDKs for Python, Node.js, and cURL. Get started in minutes.

Python 
import agroguard

client = agroguard.Client("your-api-key")

response = client.diagnose(
    image="field_photo.jpg",
    crop="maize",
    language="ha"  # Hausa
)

print(response.diagnosis)
print(response.treatment)
