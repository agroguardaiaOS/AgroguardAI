My maize leaves are turning yellow with brown spots. What is happening?
Based on your description, this could be Northern Corn Leaf Blight (NCLB).

Symptoms: Cigar-shaped gray-green lesions on lower leaves, progressing upward.

Recommended action: Apply foliar fungicide (azoxystrobin) at first sign. Remove infected leaves. Rotate crops next season.

Features

Everything a farmer needs

Voice Interaction

Speak to AgroMind in your language. No typing needed. Perfect for farmers in the field with dirty or busy hands.

Visual Pest ID

Take a photo of any affected plant. The AI analyzes it instantly and returns diagnosis, treatment, and prevention steps.

Multilingual

Full support for Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Swahili, and English. More languages added continuously based on demand.

Expert Q&A

Ask any farming question and get expert-level answers powered by the Agroguard Intelligence knowledge engine.

Seasonal Alerts

Proactive notifications about planting windows, weather risks, and disease outbreaks in your specific region.

Offline Mode

Core features work without internet via edge AI on device. Critical for remote farming areas with poor connectivity.
