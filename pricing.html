Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my API call limit?

On the Developer plan, requests will be rate-limited. On Growth, you can enable pay-as-you-go overage at $0.001 per additional call. Enterprise plans have no limits.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or educational institutions?

Yes. We offer significant discounts for non-profits, educational institutions, and agricultural cooperatives. Contact our sales team for details.

Is there a free trial for the Growth plan?

All new accounts start with 14 days of Growth plan features free. No credit card required for the trial period.

How is AgroRobotics hardware priced?

AgroRobotics hardware is priced separately and available on Enterprise plans. Contact sales for fleet configurations, leasing options, and volume discounts.
