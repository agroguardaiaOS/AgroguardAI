Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

AgroGuardAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website (agroguardai.com), API services, AgroMind application, and AgroRobotics hardware (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, organization, and password when you create an account.
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment details processed securely through our third-party payment provider.
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us.
  • Farm Data: Crop images, field coordinates, soil data, and other agricultural information you submit through our Services.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: API call logs, feature usage patterns, and interaction data.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address.
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from IP address; precise GPS coordinates only when explicitly provided for field operations.
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for authentication and preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process API requests and deliver agricultural intelligence
  • Diagnose crop diseases and provide treatment recommendations
  • Coordinate autonomous hardware operations
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Send service updates and security notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our AI models (only with aggregated, anonymized data -- see Section 6)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our Services (cloud hosting, payment processing, email delivery).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following policies:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion request.
  • API Logs: Retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted.
  • Farm Images: Processed in real-time and deleted within 30 days unless you explicitly choose to store them.
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by tax and financial regulations (typically 7 years).
  • AgroRobotics Mission Data: Retained for 90 days, then archived or deleted based on your plan settings.

6. Farm Data Protection

We understand that agricultural data is sensitive and valuable. Special protections apply:

  • Your farm images and field data are never shared with other users or third parties.
  • We do not use your individual farm data to train our models without your explicit consent.
  • Anonymized, aggregated insights may be used to improve model accuracy (e.g., regional disease pattern detection).
  • Enterprise customers can opt out of all data aggregation.
  • Field coordinates and GPS data receive additional encryption at rest.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@agroguardai.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Our Services are operated globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses for cross-border transfers
  • Data residency options for Enterprise customers (choose your processing region)
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all API communications
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance (in progress)
  • Role-based access controls for all internal systems

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 16, we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:

  • General: info@agroguardai.com
