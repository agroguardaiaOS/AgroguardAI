Workflow

From Observation to Action

A closed-loop system where AI drives every decision from scouting to treatment execution.

01

Monitor

Satellite imagery and autonomous drones continuously survey your fields, capturing high-resolution multispectral data on demand or on schedule.

02

Analyze

AgroGuard Intelligence processes imagery in real time, identifying disease hotspots, nutrient deficiencies, pest activity, and yield risks with enterprise-grade accuracy.

03

Recommend

The AI generates actionable treatment plans with precise coordinates, dosages, timing, and ROI projections for each affected zone.

04

Execute

Autonomous robots execute the treatment plan with centimeter-level precision. No human in the loop. Results logged and verified automatically.
