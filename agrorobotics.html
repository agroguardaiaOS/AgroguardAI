Workflow

How AgroRobotics works

A closed-loop system where AI drives every decision from scouting to treatment.

01

Scout

Drones autonomously survey your fields on scheduled routes, capturing high-resolution multispectral imagery.

02

Analyze

Agroguard Intelligence processes the imagery, identifying disease hotspots, nutrient deficiencies, and pest activity.

03

Plan

The AI generates a treatment map with precise coordinates, dosages, and timing for each affected zone.

04

Execute

Sprayer drones autonomously carry out the treatment plan with centimeter-level precision. No human in the loop needed.
